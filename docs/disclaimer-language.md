# Disclaimer Language

## Short disclaimer

Educational estimate only. Not financial, lending, tax, accounting, legal, or professional advice.

## Full disclaimer

This calculator provides an educational planning estimate based on the assumptions you enter. It does not guarantee profitability, ad performance, funding approval, repayment terms, or business outcomes. It does not replace a CFO, CPA, accountant, lender, underwriter, attorney, tax professional, or human review.

## Funding-safe wording

Use “compare funding options,” “review funding options,” and “funding may be worth reviewing if unit economics are sound.”

Avoid “you should borrow,” “guaranteed approval,” “risk-free capital,” and claims that funding will solve a cash gap.
